Thong diep trung tam:
Dat Me dang bi bua vay boi 4 vet thuong o nhiem: Dat, Khi, Nuoc, Lua.
Nguoi choi vao vai Ve Binh Nam, vua chien dau vua hoc cach hoa giai tac dong cua con nguoi len tu nhien.
Nguoi choi co 2 cach choi:
1) Theo nhiem vu dan duong (Story Path)
2) Kham pha tu do (Free Path)
Du choi cach nao, dieu kien bat buoc de win van la: thu thap du EarthCrystal + WindCrystal + WaterCrystal + FireCrystal.

Quy tac tien trinh:
- Co 5 map, 5 boss.
- Map 1-4 moi map co 1 boss nguyen to va 1 crystal tuong ung.
- Chi khi hoan thanh 4 map dau (da thu du 4 crystal) moi mo khoa Map 5 de dau trum cuoi.
- Coin dung de mua vu khi va nang cap tai cua NPC Thuong Nhan Reu.
- EXP Gem dung de tang dame va max HP (thong qua he thong level).
- HealthPotion, ManaPotion, StrengthBuff, DefenseBuff la vat pham ho tro trong cac tran kho.
